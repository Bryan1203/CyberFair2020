This website uses information from the interviews with master Chin-Yi Yen and master Chia-Yi Lin. The contents of the website are not documented word by word of what the two masters said in the interviews, rather all contents used from the interview are the team members’ interpretation of the information. While writing and editing the content of our website, both master Chin-Yi Yen and master Chia-Yi Lin’s interviews were references we used.

Most pictures from this website are pictures taken by the team members of this project during the interviews with the masters, with a few pictures obtained from the internet. Links to the pictures obtained from the internet can be found in the citations.
